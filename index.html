Abdul Sattar Edhi

He was a Pakistani philanthropist, ascetic,
and humanitarian who founded the Edhi Foundation.

Abdul Sattar Edhi was born in 1928 in a small village of Bantva near Joona Garh, Gujrat (India).

He established world's largest volunteer ambulance network

He established Edhi Foundation which can provide education,children,hospital and many other services.
